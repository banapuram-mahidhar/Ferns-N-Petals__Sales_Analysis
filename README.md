# FNP Sales Analysis Project

## Company Overview

FNP (Ferns N Petals) is a gifting company offering products such as cakes, flowers, plants, sweets, soft toys, and personalized gifts for various occasions like birthdays, anniversaries, festivals, and special events.

This project focuses on analyzing sales performance to uncover revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and operational insights.


## Project Objective

## The main goal of this analysis is to:

- Understand sales and revenue trends

- Identify top-performing products and categories

- Analyze customer purchasing patterns

- Evaluate delivery efficiency

- Support business decision-making using data-driven insights
  

## Tools & Technologies Used

- Excel / Power BI – Data cleaning, modeling & dashboard creation

- Data Visualization – KPI cards, line charts, bar charts, slicers

- Business Analysis – Sales & customer behavior interpretation
  

## Key KPIs

Total Orders: 1,000

Total Revenue: ₹35,20,984

Average Customer Spending: ₹3,520.98

Average Delivery Time: 5.53 days


## Key Insights & Analysis

### Monthly Revenue Trend

- Revenue fluctuates across the year.

- Peak sales observed in February and August, indicating strong seasonal demand.

- Lower revenue months highlight opportunities for promotions or campaigns.

**Insight: Seasonal occasions significantly impact sales performance.**

### Top 5 Products by Revenue

- Harum Pack generates the highest revenue.

- Nam Gift and Et Set are also strong performers.

- Quos Box and Sed Set show comparatively lower revenue.

**Insight: Focusing inventory and marketing on top-performing products can boost revenue.**

### Revenue by Weekday

- Tuesday records the highest revenue.

- Wednesday shows the lowest sales performance.

- Weekends (Saturday & Sunday) maintain moderate sales.

**Insight: Targeted weekday offers can help improve mid-week sales.**

### Category-wise Revenue

- Colors category contributes the highest revenue.

- Cake and Mugs show good performance.

- Plants, Soft Toys, and Sweets generate lower revenue.

**Insight: Festive categories dominate sales; niche categories need better promotion.**

### Orders by City

- Cities like Bhatpara, Dhanbad, and Imphal show higher order counts.

- Some cities have relatively fewer orders, indicating untapped markets.

**Insight: Regional marketing strategies can increase orders in low-performing cities.**

### Occasion-based Revenue

- Holi alone generates significant revenue among occasions.

- Demonstrates the strong impact of festival-based gifting behavior.

**Insight: Festival-focused campaigns are crucial for revenue growth.**

### Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing spend during high-performing months and festivals

- Focus on top-selling products to maximize profitability

- Improve promotions for low-performing categories

- Optimize delivery operations to reduce average delivery time

- Launch targeted campaigns on low-sales weekdays
